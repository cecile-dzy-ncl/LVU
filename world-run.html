LVforUNICEF WORLD RUN

One day, multiple locations, hundreds of LV employees.

All around the world, LV employees will run the same day for the same cause! Challenge your coworkers or any LV staff member across the ocean to participate in this exceptional world run!

Collect donations from your friends, coworkers, and family to help raise funds for your team: the team with the most funds raised will win a special surprise!

Doing a good action is the perfect way to begin the semester! It is a great opportunity to achieve your own personal goals and raise funds for children in need all around the world.

Enjoy a post-race celebration with food, music, prizes, a photo-booth, and much more! Run, walk, unwind, network, and have fun at the LVforUNICEF World Run!

THE DIFFERENCE YOU MAKE

With 10 €, you fund the equivalent of 12,500 liters of drinkable water.

With 30 €, you fund the equivalent of 200 vaccinations against the Polio virus.

With 60 €, you fund the equivalent of 20 UNICEF backpacks for children to go to school.

With 100 €, you fund the equivalent of 300 packs of ready-to-use therapeutic food used to fight malnutrition.

See more at www.unicef.fr/thedifference
